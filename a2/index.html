How To Make A Windmill


Materials:

  • Foam sheet
  • Scissors
  • Glue Dots
  • Paper straws
  • Pen
  • Ruler
  • Pipe Cleaners
    •

    Methord:

  • 0:30 - Step 1 Cut form into a 12.5 cm square
  • 0:42 – Step 2 Fold the foam square in half diagonally to make a triangle. Unfold and refold the other diagonal
  • 0:53 - Step 3 Make a hole in the middle of the two folds using a pen
  • 1:10 - Step 4 Then use some scissors to cut along the fold lines halfway into the middle. Then poke 4 holes at the edge of the foam, one on each side of the cut you just made
  • 1:20 – Step 5 Under each edge hole place a glue dot
  • 1:38 – Step 6 Bring each corner into the middle so the holes stack on top of each other, using the glue dot to stick them together. You need to be able to stick a Pipe cleaner through the hole
  • 2:05 – Step 7 Using one of the off cuts of foam, press down on it using the lid of the glue stick to create an impression in the foam. Cut out this circle using scissors and punch a hole in the middle. Do this twice to create 2 circles.
  • 2:45 – Step 8 Wrap the pipe cleaner around the straw, so about half the pipe cleaner remains sticking out
  • 2:56 – Step 9 Thread one of the smaller foam circles onto the pipe cleaner. Then thread the foam windmill onto the pipe cleaner, add the final foam circle onto the pipe cleaner
  • 3:25 –Step 10 Cut the pipe cleaner so only about 2cm is left sticking out. Then wrap the remaking pipe cleaner around so it holds the foam circus and windmill section onto the straw.
