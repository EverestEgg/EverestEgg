    Primary Goal

    What is your impression of the intended primary goal of the interactive experience?

    The website is designed to evoke the feeling of a nice summer day. Specifically, a summer holiday at the beach. Creating a sense of nostalgia and reminiscing about the good days, even if the viewer hasn't had an experience that compares to what Mille’s is showing.

    How does the interactive experience communicate this primary goal?

    This is done through a combination of visuals and audio. With the audio doing a lot of the emotional heavy lifting, the music evokes feelings of nostalgia and calm. While the ambience is used to set the environments, using calming sounds and ones associated with the beach. I found myself often holding the music and just listening to the ambience, my favorite weas the cat purring.
